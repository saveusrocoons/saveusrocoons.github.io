Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing compassionate care and medical treatment for orphaned gay raccoons battling cancer. Our mission is to ensure every raccoon receives the love, attention, and specialized veterinary care they deserve.

Through our annual walkathon and generous donations, we fund critical treatments, rehabilitation programs, and create safe havens where these beautiful creatures can heal and thrive.
